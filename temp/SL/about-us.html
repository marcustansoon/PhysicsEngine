Background History

Since 2015, Shian Liang Petroleum Shd Bhd had being established to distribute oil products and offer oil storage-related services throughout Sarawak region. Delivering unfeigned oil products is affirmed as their main obligation at work. Hence, quality services and products' assurance are deemed as their focal point to suit customer demands. So, there is no worries for any unforseen degradation of industry machines upon usage as products' quality is guaranteed. Furthermore, all oil-related products are acquired legally from the licensed distributors.

Over the years, Shian Liang company has offered various services for businesses from different industries of both land and water sector in assisting their business operation. They also sell a wide variety of oils for uses in industrial and construction sectors. One of their best selling products is Diesel Oil.

In pursuit of customer satisfaction, they have introduced Customer Loyalty Programme as a way of engaging and benefits customers. Points are accumulated for every purchases of the products and can be utilised to redeem valuable rewards.

As customers' needs are always evolving, new possibilities and new consequences may unfolding all around us. Hence, they are always eager to take in new innovative ideas to improve their products and provide more services in future.
